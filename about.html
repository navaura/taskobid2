Journey

Our Story

TaskoBid was born from a simple frustration: in India, finding reliable service professionals is often time-consuming, unclear, and unfair for both customers and workers.

Customers usually spend hours calling multiple providers, negotiating prices blindly, and still face uncertainty around quality and availability. On the other side, skilled professionals struggle to find consistent work and are often forced to depend on middlemen who charge high commissions.

We asked a simple question — what if customers could post their requirement once, and verified professionals competed transparently for that job?

That question became TaskoBid — a bidding-based service platform where customers compare offers openly, and professionals get direct access to real work without commission cuts.

TaskoBid is currently in beta, onboarding customers and service providers, validating real-world usage, and refining the experience before wider rollout.
