3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: Create and manage your TaskoBid account
  • Service Matching: Connect customers with suitable service providers based on location, requirements, and ratings
  • Bidding System: Enable transparent bidding and pricing
  • Payments: Process secure transactions through trusted payment gateways
  • AI Improvements: Enhance system performance and recommendations using machine learning
  • Security: Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Communications: Send updates, notifications, offers, or important service changes
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries and resolve disputes
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns and improve user experience
