Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using TaskoBid

How does TaskoBid work?

Post your task with budget and location. Verified service providers place competitive bids. You choose the best bid, the job starts with OTP verification, and payment is released only after completion.

Is TaskoBid free for customers?

Yes. Posting a job, receiving bids, and selecting a provider is completely free. You only pay the provider for the service you approve.

Who can become a service provider?

Freelancers and businesses can both register as providers. Verification and KYC are mandatory to ensure safety and trust on the platform.

How is job safety ensured?

Jobs are protected using start and completion OTPs. Work officially begins and ends only after OTP verification, preventing fraud or false claims.

Are payments secure?

Yes. Payments are handled through secure, PCI-compliant gateways. Funds are released to providers only after successful job completion.

How does the wallet and withdrawal work?

Providers receive earnings directly in their TaskoBid wallet. Withdrawals are available after completing the first job and are processed within 2–3 business days.

What if there is a dispute?

TaskoBid has a structured dispute resolution system. Our support team reviews job data, OTP logs, and communication to ensure a fair outcome.

Which locations does TaskoBid support?

Currently live across West Bengal. Expansion to more cities and states is already in progress.
