FAQs

Pricing Questions Answered

Is there really no subscription?

Absolutely! No monthly fees, no yearly plans, no hidden subscriptions. Both users and service providers can use TaskoBid completely free. We only charge a small commission on completed jobs.

How does commission work?

Service providers pay a 12-15% commission only on successfully completed jobs. No commission on cancelled jobs or if the task isn't completed.

When do I pay the commission?

Commission is automatically deducted when you receive payment after job completion. You always see the final amount before accepting any task.

Are there any other fees?

No! The commission is the only fee. No registration fees, no listing fees, no bidding fees, no payment processing fees. Just the commission on completed work.

Do users pay anything?

Users never pay TaskoBid anything. Not for posting tasks, not for receiving bids, nothing. They only pay the service provider they choose for the actual service.

Can the commission rate change?

The commission rate is fixed at 12-15% for all service providers. We believe in transparency and fairness ‚Äì everyone pays the same rate.
